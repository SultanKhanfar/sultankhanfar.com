Utilized immunohistochemistry, confocal microscopy, and Sholl analysis to quantify changes in microglia morphology correlated with hypertension. Conducted Î±-SMA and Trichrome staining to investigate lung vascular remodeling. Performed flow cytometry to measure immune cell infiltration in different regions of mouse brain and lungs. Presented <a href="https://doi.org/10.1152/physiol.2024.39.S1.2611" target="_blank"> first-author abstract</a> at APS 2024.
